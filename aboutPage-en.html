ABOUT US

TIVENDA BERSAUDARA offers creative and ecological packaging solutions. Over the years, our company has developed to become a major maker of packaging, servicing clients in a variety of industries such as toys, cosmetics, and consumer products.

We take enthusiasm in providing great customer service. From the first design consultation to the final production run, our team collaborates closely with customers to ensure that each project is delivered on time and to the highest standards. Our mission is to be a trusted partner in your supply chain, offering packaging solutions that not only comply with industry rules but also propel your brand ahead.

OUR MISSION

Providing solutions for customers in responding to the dynamics of current and future challenges. Continuously adapting to technological changes as a means of improving services and solutions. Contributing to the nation's economy.

OUR VISION

To become a world-class solution-based company in the printing sector by prioritizing quality and service, to become a one-stop printing solution company.

THE HISTORY OF

TIVENDA BERSAUDARA

CV TIVENDA BERSAUDARA was established in 2014 and is engaged in Offset Printing & Packaging. Since its establishment, we have experience in handling promotional printing production to packaging printing. Starting from the scale of MSMEs and Multinational Companies have collaborated with Tivenda Bersaudara.

By upholding the philosophy of Evolve & Beyond, Tivenda Bersaudara continuously innovates product services by adopting technological developments. Tivenda Bersaudara wants to continue to be present in every journey of MSME growth in Indonesia, as well as becoming a printing business solution partner for Multinational Companies.
